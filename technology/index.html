Technology.

At Athelas we've built deep learning algorithms and microfluidic technology to create a truly intelligent diagnostic platform. Our tech is trained through Convolutional Neural Networks on morphology data to classify cell shapes and detect abnormalities. Using TensorFlow and image processing, Athelas does high resolution imaging on a drop of blood and generates a blood count report for rapid PoC diagnostics.


The technology lies at the confluence of biotechnology and machine learning, using proven data science to generate a lab-grade Complete Blood Count.Synthesized with symptomatic data, Athelas becomes an incredibly powerful diagnostic platform in point of care, home, and hospital settings.

Our microfluidic strip collects a precise volume of blood, and interfaces it with the correct components to generate a statistically representative sample of a human's current biological health. Capillary flow enables consistent sampling, and the interfacing algorithms utilize data science to then capture the White Blood Cell differntial levels along with morphology.

In cohesion, the microfluidic sampling, deep learning image processing, and morphological analysis create a truly intelligent diagnostic platform, capable of augmenting in-hospital intelligence within a few seconds.
