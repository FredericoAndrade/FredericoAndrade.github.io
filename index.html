Our technology.

We've developed state of the art Natural Language Processing algorithms to understand and model information. Clipped analyzes an article and determines its most relevant points, ultimately generating an intelligent, coherent summary for the reader.

As opposed to previous approaches at summarization, which focused heavily on sentence ranking and keyword frequencies, Clipped leverages grammatical patterns. This unique trait allows it to determine the relationship/role of words in their context, and generate automatic infographics from the article.

A really smart news reader.

Clipped for iOS is a beautiful, simple, and intelligent news reading experience. Select the categories you want to read from, and immediately get quick summarized articles that have just the information you need to know.

Our summarization technology coupled with infographic extraction results in a beautiful experience that finds the important stuff and presents it in a vibrant, eye-catching way.
